How I work

A multidimensional approach

01
Systemic therapy
Exploring how your relationships, resources and values shape your emotional health. By understanding these patterns, you can choose if you want to shift them.
02
Winnicottian psychoanalysis
Working with early emotional experiences and pre-verbal development. Helping you reconnect with a more authentic sense of self by understanding where it was formed.
03
Mindfulness · EFT · Schema
Evidence-based tools for emotional regulation, grounding in the present moment, and identifying and changing deep-rooted patterns of thought and behaviour.
About me

Brazilian psychologist,
In Berlin since 2010

I graduated from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ) in 2007. I worked with teenagers and families at the European School in Berlin for over 10 years and currently specialise in Systemic Therapy at the Systemisches Institut für Achtsamkeit (SIA).

My practice brings together psychoanalytic depth, relational focus, and grounding techniques — with a committed decolonial approach. My work in arts, music, and performance has given me a distinctive sensitivity and listening ability.
